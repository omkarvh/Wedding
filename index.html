Our Love Story

First Meet

15 May 2024

It all began on a memorable day when I visited her home with my relatives. Nervous yet excited, I stepped into her world, not knowing that this moment would change my life forever. From the warm welcome to the heartfelt conversations, there was an unspoken connection that instantly felt special. It was in that very first meeting that I realized how extraordinary she is, and how perfectly our paths were meant to cross.

First Date

1 June 2024

Our first date was unlike any other—it wasn’t just the two of us, but both our families coming together to make it truly special. It was a beautiful mix of laughter, shared stories, and heartfelt moments as two families began to bond. Amidst the conversations and smiles, we found ourselves discovering more about each other, surrounded by love and blessings. That day wasn’t just a first date—it was the beginning of a beautiful journey for both of us.

Proposal

26 Jun 2024

It was a moment straight out of a dream. With our hearts already entwined, the time felt right to take the next big step. Surrounded by love and anticipation, I expressed what I had felt from the very beginning—that she is the one I want to spend my life with. Her smile said it all before her words did, and in that magical moment, our journey of forever truly began.

Enagagement

03 July 2024

A day filled with love, laughter, and the warmth of our closest family and friends—it was the perfect beginning to our forever. As we exchanged rings, we also exchanged promises to stand by each other through every moment life has to offer. Surrounded by blessings and joy, our engagement was not just a celebration of us but also a beautiful union of two families. It marked the start of a lifetime of togetherness, love, and cherished memories.
